The HTTP UserAgent Cleaner extension found that in the FireFox is enabled the e10s technology (multiprocess FireFox). For the extension work you need to disable e10s. Disable E10S may adversely affect a tabs isolation, what can affect the browser security. About E10S you can read on the page http://wiki.mozilla.org/Electrolysis . You can disable the e10s in the extension settings. After the disable E10S you need to restart the FireFox.
